Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

  1. 01

    Who This Applies To

    This policy explains how Chen Ying Yu Dorina collects, uses, and protects your personal data, in compliance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

  2. 02

    What Data Is Collected

    • Your name and contact number, shared via WhatsApp or this website
    • Health information you share, such as injuries, medical conditions, or fitness history
    • Session notes relevant to your programme
  3. 03

    Why It Is Collected

    • To schedule and conduct Pilates sessions
    • To design a programme appropriate for your body and goals
    • To communicate about bookings and cancellations
  4. 04

    How It Is Stored

    Your data is stored securely on personal devices. It is not shared with any third parties, except where required by law.

  5. 05

    How Long It Is Kept

    Data is retained for as long as you are an active client, and for up to 2 years after your last session. You may request deletion at any time.

  6. 06

    Your Rights

    Under the PDPA, you have the right to access the personal data held about you, correct any inaccurate data, withdraw consent to its use, and request that it be deleted. To exercise any of these rights, contact Dorina directly via WhatsApp.

  7. 07

    Contact

    For any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact Dorina directly via WhatsApp.

  8. 08

    Changes to This Policy

    This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will always be available on this website.
