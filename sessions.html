How It Works

Private One-on-One Sessions

The Attuned Practice offers private Pilates sessions only.

Each session is 50 minutes long and takes place in a home studio setting in Bukit Timah, Singapore, near Beauty World MRT Station. Sessions may be conducted in English or Chinese.

The home studio is equipped with a Merrithew Rehab V2 Max Reformer with Tower Frame, along with a range of props to support different bodies, levels, and goals.

Getting Started

What to Expect

Exploration

The first session is exploratory, where the instructor will observe posture, alignment, movement patterns, and comfort levels through a few basic movements. This helps shape future sessions around the client's needs, goals, and current ability.

What to Wear & Bring

Wear form-fitting sports attire, such as tights and a slim-fitting t-shirt. This allows posture, alignment, and movement to be observed more clearly. Sticky socks or normal socks must be worn during the session.

Bring water and a towel if needed. Most clients are unlikely to perspire heavily in the first few sessions, as early sessions focus on technique, body awareness, and good movement patterns rather than intensity.
